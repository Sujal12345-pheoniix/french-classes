Meet Our Founder

Unnati Vatwani is a passionate French language educator with over 7 years of experience in teaching French to students of all ages. Her journey began with a deep love for the French language and culture, which she now shares with hundreds of students across India.

With international certifications and a unique teaching methodology that combines traditional learning with modern interactive techniques, Unnati has successfully created a nurturing environment where learning French becomes an enjoyable and rewarding experience.

Our Mission

At French Classes by Unnati Vatwani, we believe that learning a new language should be an exciting adventure, not a daunting task. Our mission is to make French learning accessible, enjoyable, and effective for everyone, regardless of their age or background.
